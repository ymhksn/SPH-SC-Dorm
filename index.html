DORMLIFE
Boarding Experience at PHH Dorm
Extraordinary Dorm Team & Family
With caring dorm parents and supervisors and comprehensive dorm programs, students are guided in their journey to become independent, responsible, and compassionate young adults. Our team is responsible for the care and well-being of residential students by providing a safe, secure, nurturing, and structured dormitory experience for all boarding students. They will help address individual student needs, from academic to spiritual, supporting students as they adjust to and manage independent living.
Our dormitory is not just a place to stay but also a home to excel and gain life skills that will serve a good purpose as students move into university life and beyond. The facilities are not just limited to the dorm; the extended facilities at school add to an overall experience for every dorm student.
On-Campus Activities & Facilities
Devotions and Family Nights
Students participate in devotions once every week. Divided into small groups and led by the dorm supervisors, students share about their lives and their faith journeys. Every Wednesday is Family Night, where everyone comes together for a special dinner, group fellowship, and fun games.
24/7 Healthcare Support
We also provide an on-campus nurse and essential medical assistance for all dormitory students. Yet if a more severe matter arises, the nurse will be alert and call for hospital intervention.
English Tutoring Sessions
As some students have limited proficiency in the English language, we provide additional English support for PHH residents, which helps them adjust to an English-speaking environment.
Healthy and Nutritious Diet
We acknowledge that every teenager needs healthy, nutritious, and delicious food to support their active lives. It provides three meals daily and accommodates allergies and dietary needs.
Safe and secure environment
Our security team ensures the safety and security of the dormitory and campus 24/7. They monitor all access to community members and approved guests.
